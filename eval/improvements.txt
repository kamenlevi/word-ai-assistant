});

**L1-section-break-001**
```javascript
await Word.run(async (context) => {
  await context.document.body.insertSectionBreak("continuous");
  await context.document.body.setPageOrientation("landscape");
  await context.document.body.sections.getItem(context.document.body.sections.length - 1).setPageOrientation("landscape");
  await context.sync();
});
```
